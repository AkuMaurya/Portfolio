Galanto

3D Games (Developed at Galanto Innovation)

Scooter driving simulation

  • Perfect for scooter driving practice.
  • A functional exercise designed to simulate the wrist and finger movements involved in scooter driving.
  • It focuses on wrist flexion and extension, as well as finger grip and release, aiming to train range of motion and motor coordination.
  • This type of exercise is beneficial for individuals undergoing rehabilitation to improve their upper limb functionality.
  • Self made environment and Scooter model.
  • Realistic controls.
  • Hold and accelerate with hand to drive.

Pick And Place

  • This Exercise is for elbow shoulder and finger movement.
  • Here we need to segregate the Objects.

GalaxyWarrior

  • This game focus for for elbow training.
  • Here we need to save the ship by getting collide with enemy & collect the coin.

Kick-It

  • After every 10 seconds of interval a random zombie will appear.
  • With leg perform action of kicking to shoot a ball and kill the zombie.
  • Once you kill 50 zombie you will unlock new level.

AeroFill

  • We need to fill air in empty tire of cycle, motor bike, and other vehicals seen in the screen.
  • Different number of repetations needs to be done in order to fill air in tube.
  • Once you fill the tyre, you will get the reward.

Balloon Shooting

  • Ten balloons appear on the board in random colors.
  • At a time, one balloon is highlighted, and the player must shoot it.
  • Shooting is done using a hardware gripper module by holding it in hand and applying pressure.
  • Once all balloons burst, a new set of randomly colored balloons appears on the board.

Fruit Slash

  • An orange fruit pops up at a random position along the x-axis on the screen.
  • When the fruit is highlighted, the player must slash it.
  • Slashing is performed by wearing an arm hardware module.
  • If the patient does not have sufficient range of motion, the range can be scaled so that it covers the entire screen and allows the fruit to be cut.
  • Fruits continue to appear every 3–5 seconds.

Wood Cutting

  • Logs of wood keep appearing, and the player needs to cut them one by one.
  • The higher the hand is raised, the more pieces the wood is cut into.
  • Wood cutting is performed by wearing an arm hardware module.
  • Here as well, the patient’s movement range can be scaled according to the game.

Clean Up

  • This activity trains coordinated wrist and finger movements.
  • Finger movements enable the user to grip and control the scrubber.
  • Wrist movements drive realistic floor-cleaning actions.
