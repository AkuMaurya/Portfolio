Scooter driving simulation

  • Perfect for scooter driving practice.
  • A functional exercise designed to simulate the wrist and finger movements involved in scooter driving.
  • It focuses on wrist flexion and extension, as well as finger grip and release, aiming to train range of motion and motor coordination.
  • This type of exercise is beneficial for individuals undergoing rehabilitation to improve their upper limb functionality.
  • Self made environment and Scooter model.
  • Realistic controls.
  • Hold and accelerate with hand to drive.
